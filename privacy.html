Privacy

How we hold your
information.

susurre is a small, private scent house. We collect only what we need, share nothing we don't have to, and let you leave whenever you wish.

Last updated · April 26, 2026

What we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your first name, if you choose to share it

When you visit susurre.com, we collect anonymous analytics — page views, country-level location, device type, and referrer. We do not use cookies for tracking, and we never tie this data to your identity.

How we use it

We use your email for one purpose: to tell you when susurre is ready. That means:

  • One launch announcement when Chapter One arrives
  • Occasional notes from the founder during the resolving process — never more than once a month
  • Early access offers exclusive to waitlist members

That is the entirety of our use. We will never sell, rent, or share your email with anyone.

Where it lives

Your email is stored with Beehiiv, our newsletter provider. Anonymous site analytics are processed by Vercel. Both providers are bound by their own privacy commitments — you can read them at beehiiv.com/privacy and vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

We do not share your data with anyone outside of these technical providers.

Your rights

You can leave the waitlist at any time:

  • Unsubscribe using the link at the bottom of any email we send
  • Request deletion of your data by emailing hello@susurre.com — we will remove your information within seven days
  • Request a copy of the data we hold on you, also by emailing us

If you are in the European Union, the United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA. We honor all such requests.

Children

susurre is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16. If you believe we have, please write to us and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

If anything material changes, we will note the new date above and tell waitlist members directly. The current version always lives here.

Contact

For any question about your data — or anything else — write to hello@susurre.com. A real person will reply.

Thank you for trusting us with your inbox.
— susurre
